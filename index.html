FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I earn daily returns? +

After investing, you need to complete one daily task — watch a short video — to unlock your 5% daily return. Without completing the task, returns are not credited for that day.

What is the minimum investment? +

The minimum investment plan starts at GHS 150. There is no minimum deposit to open an account.

When can I withdraw my money? +

Withdrawals are processed every Thursday. Minimum withdrawal is GHS 30. A 1.4% processing fee is deducted from your withdrawal amount.

What is the referral program? +

When you refer a friend and they make their first deposit, you earn 5% of their deposit amount — credited instantly to your balance.

What is SaxoTrader? +

SaxoTrader is an advanced account for professional traders. It requires a Trader ID assigned by Saxo administration. For regular investors, we recommend starting with SaxoInvestor.

Which MoMo networks are supported? +

We support MTN MoMo and Telecel Cash for both deposits and withdrawals. The correct account is assigned automatically based on your network.
