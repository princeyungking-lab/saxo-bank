Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

📅 Last updated: March 2025
Summary: We collect only what we need to run your account. We never sell your data to third parties. Your information stays in Ghana.

1. Who We Are

Saxo Bank is a Ghana-based investment platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our platform at saxo-bank-gh.netlify.app (or our custom domain).

Contact: [email protected] — Accra, Ghana

2. Information We Collect

When you register and use Saxo Bank, we collect:

  • Full name — to identify your account
  • Mobile phone number — used as your account ID and for MoMo transactions
  • Email address — optional, used for account communications
  • Password — stored securely to protect your account
  • MoMo number — used for deposit and withdrawal processing
  • Transaction history — deposits, withdrawals, investments, profits
  • Device information — browser type, for security purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your investment account
  • Process your deposits and withdrawals via MoMo
  • Credit daily profits to your balance
  • Send you important account notifications via SMS
  • Process referral bonuses
  • Respond to your support messages
  • Prevent fraud and protect all users
  • Comply with applicable laws in Ghana

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore, a globally trusted cloud database service. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If your account is deleted, your personal information is removed from our active database within 30 days.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

We only share your information with:

  • MTN Ghana / Telecel Ghana — solely for processing your MoMo transactions
  • Arkesel — our SMS provider, for sending you account notifications
  • EmailJS — for sending email communications to our admin
  • Law enforcement — if required by Ghanaian law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected].

7. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We use:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Google Firebase security rules to restrict data access
  • Password protection on all accounts
  • Manual verification of all deposits before crediting

However, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and never share your account details with anyone.

8. Cookies

Saxo Bank does not use tracking cookies. We use browser localStorage only to remember your login session and preferences (such as your last visited page). This data stays on your device and is never sent to third parties.

9. Children's Privacy

Saxo Bank is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover that a minor has registered, we will immediately delete their account.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via SMS or email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
