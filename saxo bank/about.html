About Saxo Bank

Ghana's trusted daily investment platform — built to help everyday Ghanaians grow their money simply and reliably.

Who We Are

Saxo Bank is a Ghana-based investment platform that allows individuals to grow their savings through daily returns. We are based in Accra, Ghana and operate exclusively within the Ghanaian market, accepting payments through MTN MoMo and Telecel Cash.

We were founded with a simple mission: to make investment accessible to every Ghanaian — not just the wealthy few. Whether you have GHS 150 or GHS 1,000, you can start earning daily returns with Saxo Bank.

How It Works

Saxo Bank operates on a simple model:

  • You deposit funds into your account via MTN MoMo or Telecel Cash
  • You choose an investment plan (GHS 150 — GHS 1,000)
  • Your investment earns 5% daily returns for 30 days
  • Profits are credited to your balance every midnight
  • You can withdraw your earnings every Thursday to your MoMo

A 1.4% withdrawal processing fee is deducted from your withdrawal amount. There are no other hidden fees.

Our Referral Program

We reward our community. When you refer a friend to Saxo Bank and they make their first deposit, you earn a 5% referral bonus of their deposit amount — credited instantly to your balance. There is no limit to how many people you can refer.
